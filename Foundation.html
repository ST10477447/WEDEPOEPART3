WHAT IS YOHANAN FOUNDATION

    Yohanan Foundation is a compassionate community-driven initiative dedicated to uplifting vulnerable families. The foundation currently provides essential support through food parcels and clothing donations, helping to meet basic needs with dignity. Looking ahead, Yohanan Foundation aims to launch a daycare center to offer safe, nurturing care for young children—empowering parents to pursue work or education while ensuring their children thrive.

    MISSION STATEMENT

    Through the legacy of the late Prophet Yohanan Uche Akwada by surrounding support to the poor, spreading spiritual wisdom. Educating through impactful teaching of the word of God, and promoting good health and holistic healing to enhance the lives of individuals and communities.

    VISION STATEMENT

    Supporting a world inspired by the teachings of late Prophet Yohanan Uche Akwada, where every person experiences the love of God,the power of healing and the hope of a brighter future, transforming lives through love, faith and charity.
